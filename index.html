About Me

Kristjan is CEO & Co-Founder of 50skills.com - the people process automation platform for seamless employee journeys and enhanced productivity.

50skills is a software solution that amplifies HR managers to automate their people processes. It's a no-code employee journey builder, that increases productivity and retention. It's lightning fast and integrates with anything employers use, bringing custom AI services such as OpenAI GPT's to any process.

Journeys by 50skills provides templates for every people process, from onboarding to offboarding, paperwork, training, and integrations, helping businesses optimize HR operations.

Before 50skills, Kristjan was VP of Marketing Products at Meniga (2014-2017). Prior to that, he led Klak and Startup Reykjavik, contributing to the entrepreneurial scene in Iceland. He has facilitated global startup events, delivered keynotes, and taught over 1,000 students at various universities.

Kristjan's educational background includes a Bachelor's in Political Science (with economics emphasis) and a Master's in Marketing from the University of Iceland.
